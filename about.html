SYNESTHETIC PERCEPTIONS

ABOUT

Synesthetic Perceptions is an exploration of the relationships between visual and auditory composition.

PART I

Interpretations

A series of musical compositions and their synesthetic interpretations.

PART II

Exhibits

Using tangible items as mediums to exhibitionize the musivisual parallelisms and design theories driving the aformentioned interpretations.
